Specializing in Car Accident and Personal Injury Cases

Board-certified personal injury and car accident attorney Shane Kadlec serves victims of personal injury and car accidents throughout Houston, TX. We understand your full range of needs, including legal assistance, medical care, and emotional support. With more than 20 years of experience, Shane is certified in personal injury law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization.

Fewer than 10 percent of all attorneys who practice personal injury law have earned this distinction. It requires significant trial experience, rigorous testing and a positive peer evaluation of the attorney’s abilities. This is a single-attorney practice, which means Shane is engaged directly with every client.

By limiting his caseload, he provides the hands-on attention personal injury and accident cases require in seeking the best possible outcomes.

One of the first things crossing an injury victim’s mind is how much it will cost to piece their lives back together.
